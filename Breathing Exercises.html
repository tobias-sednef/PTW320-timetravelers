Breathing Exercises

4-7-8 Breathing


To begin this exercise, sit or lay down with the back straight. Place the tip of your tongue against the tissue just above your upper front teeth and keep it there throughout the exercise.

  1. Breathe through your nose for four seconds
  2. Hold your breath for seven seconds
  3. Exhale through your mouth for eight seconds

*Repeat the above cycle four times.

Belly Breathing

To begin this exercise, you can either sit or lay down in a comfortable position.

  1. Place one hand on your stomach and the other hand on your chest. Imagine there is a balloon in your stomach that you are filling (by expanding the stomach) and deflating (by gently contracting the abdominal muscles).
  2. Breathe through your nose for a slow count of five. You should feel your stomach rise as you inhale.
  3. Exhale slowly over a count of five. You should feel the balloon in your stomach deflating. The hand on your chest should remain mostly still.
